Project Overview

This project was assigned by our client with the objective of developing a fully functional portfolio website along with an integrated admin portal. The portfolio website is designed to showcase his professional profile, including sections such as About Me, experience, education, research work, publications, blogs, collaborators, and contact details.

It serves as a comprehensive platform to present all his achievements and contributions, particularly highlighting his research papers and blog posts in an organized and accessible manner.

The website includes dynamic features such as user authentication, allowing visitors to sign up and log in to access private research papers. Users can interact with the platform by viewing and commenting on blog posts. A chat/contact system is also integrated, enabling visitors to send messages directly through the website — helping them connect with him easily.

Portfolio Website — Key Features

  • Comprehensive profile sections: About Me, Experience, Education, Research, Publications, Blogs, Collaborators & Contact
  • User authentication — sign up and log in to access private research papers
  • Interactive blog section with commenting system for audience engagement
  • Integrated chat/contact system for direct visitor communication
  • Research section with detailed insights into all completed work

Admin Portal — Full Control

  • Complete CRUD functionality — Create, Read, Update, Delete all website content
  • Publish and manage research papers and blog posts with full editorial control
  • View and manage visitor messages received through the contact system
  • LinkedIn integration — publish content simultaneously on portfolio and LinkedIn
  • Edit all portfolio sections in real-time — full control over what to post and when
