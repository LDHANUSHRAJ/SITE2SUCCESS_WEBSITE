What is KIRA?

KIRA is an AI engagement platform that businesses train on their own data — website content, PDFs, FAQs, service menus — and deploy across multiple customer touchpoints. It handles customer queries, captures leads, books appointments, and manages follow-ups — without human intervention.

The platform is trained on your own business data, so it responds like a knowledgeable staff member, not a generic bot. Everything from lead tracking to analytics to multi-channel management lives in a single dashboard.

🚀 New Feature

Outbound Engagement Engine

Instead of waiting for customers to come to you, KIRA actively goes where your potential customers are already having conversations online. KIRA monitors platforms like Reddit, LinkedIn, Facebook, and X/Twitter for discussions relevant to your business — and replies with helpful, branded responses that bring them to your door.

Step 1

Detects Intent

KIRA scans Reddit, LinkedIn, Facebook posts, and X/Twitter threads for questions and discussions relevant to your business.

Step 2

Replies Smartly

Crafts a helpful, branded response with a natural call to action — not spammy, genuinely useful to the person asking.

Step 3

Drives to Site

Includes a link to your landing page or WhatsApp CTA, bringing interested prospects directly to your business.

Step 4

Stores Lead

Logs who they are, which platform, what they asked, and their contact details — all structured in your KIRA dashboard.

Think of it as having a social media SDR running 24/7 — finding people mid-thought, responding at exactly the right moment, and delivering them to your door already informed and interested.
