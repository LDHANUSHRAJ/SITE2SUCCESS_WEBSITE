About Site2Success

Site2Success is an AI-first technology startup headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India, incubated at Christ University. Founded in October 2024 by co-founders Rahul R (CEO) and L Dhanush Raj (COO), the company was built on a single mission: make cutting-edge AI accessible and actionable for Indian small and medium businesses (SMBs).

Site2Success specialises in building agentic AI systems, autonomous AI agents, conversational chatbots, intelligent business automation, and AI-powered workflow orchestration for Indian SMBs. The company's flagship product, KIRA, is an agentic automation AI designed specifically for Indian SMB workflows — currently in early access.

The company serves forward-thinking Indian businesses seeking AI-driven transformation. Whether it's deploying autonomous agents that handle leads, automating multi-step business workflows, or building AI-integrated business systems — Site2Success delivers results at startup-friendly pricing with enterprise-grade quality.

Based in Bengaluru — India's Silicon Valley — Site2Success is positioned at the forefront of India's agentic AI revolution, serving the digital needs of 63 million SMBs. Contact Site2Success at site2successs@gmail.com or call +91 6361446768.
